Frequently Asked Questions

Is this raffle legal?

Yes — we operate under a verified promotional raffle license. No purchase is required to enter.

How many times can I enter?

One entry per person/email. Multiple entries from the same person will be removed.

When will the draw happen?

We draw once every week.

How do winners get notified?

Via phone call + email within 48 hours.

Is my data safe?

Yes — All information submitted to this site is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Fun fact?

The word “raffle” comes from the Dutch word “rafelen” meaning “to tangle” — because entries get deliciously mixed in the pool!
